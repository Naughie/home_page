Theorem 2.1

  • Statement
  • Proof

\(c \defeq \sum c_n (z - a)^n \in \C \llbracket z - a \rrbracket\), \(\rho \defeq\) radius of convergence of \(c\). \[ \begin{alignat*}{0} f \colon &\Delta_\rho (a) &&\longrightarrow &&\C \\ &\style{transform: rotate(270deg)}{\in} && &&\style{transform: rotate(270deg)}{\in} \\ &z &&\longmapsto &&\sum c_n (z - a)^n \end{alignat*} \] とおく．ただし， \[ \Delta_\rho (a) = \{ z \mid |z - a| < \rho \}. \]

Then,

  • \(f\) は holomorphic on \(|z - a| < \rho\) で，its derivative は \(f' (z) = \sum (n + 1) c_{n + 1} ( z - a )^n\)．

Corollary 2.2

  • Statement
  • Proof

\(c \defeq \sum c_n (z - a)^n \in \C \llbracket z - a \rrbracket\), \(\rho \defeq\) radius of convergence of \(c\). \[ \begin{alignat*}{0} f \colon &\Delta_\rho (a) &&\longrightarrow &&\C \\ &\style{transform: rotate(270deg)}{\in} && &&\style{transform: rotate(270deg)}{\in} \\ &z &&\longmapsto &&\sum c_n (z - a)^n \end{alignat*} \] とおく．

Then,

  • \(f\) は \(\C\)-\(C^\infty\) on \(|z - a| < \rho\)．
